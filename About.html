OUR VISION

Releasing the Fire of Christ for Transformation unto Christlikeness and Promoting the Unity of All Christians, not minding the differences, for Heaven on Earth, and into the next, if Jesus tarries. (John 17:21, John 10:10, Ephesians 4:11-16).

OUR TASKS

  • Bringing the lost souls and backsliders to Jesus
  • Fathering, Teaching, Evangelism, Pastoring, Preaching, Prayers, Warfare, and Prophetic Ministrations
  • Recruiting soldiers of Christ for spiritual warfare
  • Training leaders of leaders
  • Counseling and deliverance
  • Church planting when the need arises
  • Translations and publications
  • Giving vocational training that leads to self-reliance

OUR PROGRAM

Our nature is The Fivefold Ministries and our programs are interdenominational, unless on specification. The programs are: Crusades, Conferences, outreaches, Seminars, Retreats, workshops, Intercessory prayer/warfare meeting, Interdenominational breaking of bread, Mercy Hour, Prophetic and Discovering Bible Study(Discipleship Making Movement)
